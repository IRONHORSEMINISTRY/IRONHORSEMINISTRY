Our Mission

Iron Horse Ministry is a faith-based ministry committed to living out and teaching biblical principles of stewardship, community, and righteousness. We serve families, rural communities, and those who labor in the field, both literally and spiritually. Our focus includes land restoration, faith-based education, and practical service.

Statement of Faith
